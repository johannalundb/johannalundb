Compassion Scent

A concept for a perfume and campaign to prevent the negative pictures which are created when a group of people / country is in crisis. Because of the massive media consumption of negative pictures I want to bring awareness to people in need of help in a more constructive way by portraying them from a more another perspective. The design & scent is inspired by the nature and colors of Syriah.

"This is not just a normal perfume.
It is a scent of compassion.
The purpose with this scent is to create something beautiful, inspired by the country itself, in the middle of a crisis. In order to help and bring awareness and compassion to this country and its people.
We reject labeling these people and choose to keep what is sacred about them, about all of us; we are all humans and we are all bound together.
This perfume is a symbol for compassion.
All of the economic profit goes to help the victims."
