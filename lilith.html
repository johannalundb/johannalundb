WHO IS LILITH?

In Jewish folklore Lilith appears as Adam’s first wife, who was created equally at the same time and from the same dirt as Adam. He wanted Lilith to be his subservient, but she refused, and left him. Since Lilith’s position is beyond male control, she’s been portrayed evil and demonized. Lilith is the symbol for female sexuality and real female power.
