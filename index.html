perfume concept

Idea for a perfume

"This is not just a normal perfume; it is a scent of compassion.

The purpose with this scent is to create something beautiful, inspired by the country itself, in the middle of a crisis. In order to help and bring awareness and compassion to this country and its people.

We reject labeling these people and choose to keep what is sacred about them, about all of us; we are all humans and we are all bound together.

This perfume is a symbol for compassion.

All of the economic profit goes to help the victims."
